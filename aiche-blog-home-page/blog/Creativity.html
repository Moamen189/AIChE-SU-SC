Creativity

Every one of us has ambitions and dreams And always tries to achieve them. Every one wants to be successful. But how? You need to generate better ideas than others. You should have distinguish thinking. All of that we call "creativity".

Creativity, which is considered one of the most required skills, is the act of turning new and imaginative ideas into reality.

Creativity is characterised by the ability to perceive the world in new ways, to find hidden patterns, to make connections between seemingly unrelated phenomena, and to generate solutions. Creativity involves two processes: thinking, then producing So creativity is a process, which means that it has steps, it has reasons and results.

Creativity consists of 3 main steps.

1-Predisposition: which is the first step, at which you have to set your purpose, choose the most suitable field with your interests and collect as much information as you can about this field. That enables you be up to date with all lattest ideas at this field.in another way it is How to collect and analyze all data and information, take notes and manage dialogue and discussions

2-At second step, which called brewing, you start to get many ideas and purify that ideas. Brewing the idea here and then floating from time to time on the mind of the individual and feel that it is coming

3-Now it's the role of third stage, inspiration, . Now that he has reached the pinnacle of his creativity and innovation, the whole idea has shone on his mind. At that moment, everything is in place

At inspiration you apply that ideas to get an precise achievement. In that step you gain the fruit of that creative thinking.

What is the relation between ceativity and intelligence?

The relation between ceativity and intelligence is not intrinsical, which means that not every intelligent one is creative one. And not every creative person depends only his intelligence but also his information and previous experiences.

But how to be a creative person?

First of all is reading at different subjects.
Reading change the way in which you think and expand your horizons.

Second to know about others' creative models. Not only to know thier ideas, but also to learn from their failures. and relate between their interests and their ideas.

Third, perseverance
At this we must mention that the real failing person is the one who doesn't learn from his failures .

Fourth is motivation,
Always motivate yourself and create different competitions.

Fifth is curiosity,
Which means not to miss any chance to know about something.

Sixth,
strange ideas Creative person always see the possibility of applying some ideas which normal people see it impossible

Constraints of creativity.

1-refusing someone's ideas only because he is in a less rank than you.

2-not to supply creative person with required support which make hime feel unrewarded

3-The absence of self confidence which lead to ideas deterioration with no achievement.

Creative youth are considered the future of any country and the most ones who deserve the real support.

Fosters of creativity

  • Establishing purpose and intention
  • Stimulating and rewarding curiosity and exploration
  • Building motivation, especially internal motivation
  • Encouraging confidence and a willingness to take risks
  • Time management
